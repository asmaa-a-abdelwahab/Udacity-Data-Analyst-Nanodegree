III. Dog Stages¶

I analyzed the specific "dog stages", excluding the missing values to make a pie chart about the proportions of the categories. The most often used stage is "pupper" with more than 65%, the second one is "doggo" (about 22%). The account owners use the words puppo and floofer in 10% of the cases alltogether.

Conclusions¶

I got some interesting finding related to the Twitter account WeRateDogs. Among those findings were the most popular dog names, the most beloved dog breeds, percentage of dog stages, the most rated breeds, the relation between rating and vote count for the top breeds and discovering that there is no correlation between rating and favorite count and retweet count.
